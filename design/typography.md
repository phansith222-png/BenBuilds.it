# Typography & Visual Concept

## Visual Concept

"Modern Split-Screen Minimal" — Apple-like, editorial.
- **Desktop:** 50/50 horizontal split (`w-1/2` each panel)
- **Mobile:** Stacked vertically — gray section on top, black below

Contrast between Cool Gray (`#D9D9D9`) and Deep Black (`#000000`) is the core visual statement.

## Font

**Poppins** (weights 300–800) — configured in `src/app/layout.tsx`.

## Hierarchy

| Level | Usage | Tailwind |
|---|---|---|
| Hero heading | Extremely large, tight tracking | `tracking-tight`, solid `line-height` |
| Body | Clean, highly legible | `leading-relaxed` |

## Rules

- Typography is the primary visual element — no border clutter
- Let negative space breathe
- No unnecessary background shapes or decorative dividers
